Jinyu LI

PhD in Phonetics

Hi! Welcome to my page!


I currently work as a postdoctoral researcher within the project PASDCODE (Prosody AS a Dynamic COordinative DEvice). I finished my PhD at Laboratoire de Phonétique et Phonologie (LPP, CNRS/Sorbonne Nouvelle, Paris, France) under the supervision of Cécile Fougeron and Leonardo Lancia in 2023.

My research interests center around the perception-production relationship and adaptation in speech production. I am also interested in speech rhythm and psycholinguistics. My PhD dissertation focuses on how speech rhythm adapts to different speech production situations, i.e., those where speakers' auditory feedback is altered. Furthermore, I explore how speakers' sense of agency relative to their voice is affected by the speech production situation.

I have a bachelor degree in French Linguistics, Literature, and Civilization, a master degree in Language Acquisition and Pathology, and another master degree in Français Langue Étrangère (French for French as a foreign language, FLE).


KEY WORDS: Sensorimotor control, auditory feedback, phonetic alignement, speech rhythm, prosody, sense of agency, psycholinguistics, speech processing
